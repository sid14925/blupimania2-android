# Privacy Policy

**App:** BlupiMania 2 for Android (`org.blupi.blupimania2`)
**Developer:** Vilmos Cseke
**Contact:** csekevilmos@gmail.com
**Last updated:** 17 July 2026

## Summary

This app does **not** collect, store, use, share or transmit any personal data.

## Details

* **No data collection.** The app does not gather any personal or sensitive user data of
  any kind — no names, e-mail addresses, contacts, photos, files, location or device
  identifiers.
* **No network access.** The app does not request the `INTERNET` permission and does not
  communicate with any server. Nothing leaves your device.
* **No analytics, no advertising, no tracking.** The app contains no analytics SDKs, no
  advertising libraries and no crash-reporting services.
* **No third-party sharing.** Since no data is collected, no data is shared with anyone.
* **No accounts.** The app has no registration, login or user accounts.
* **Local storage only.** The app stores your game progress (player profile, solved
  puzzles, settings) in its own private application storage on your device. This data
  never leaves the device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.
* **Children.** The app is safe for all ages: as it collects no data, it collects no data
  from children either.

## Permissions

The app requests no runtime permissions.

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published on this page.
